01 — Workflow

Three keystrokes from typo to polished.

No copy-paste. No tab-switching. The popup floats above your work and disappears the moment you're done.

STEP 01

Select your text

Highlight a sentence, paragraph, or whole draft in any application — email, chat, document, or browser.

↳ works via Windows UI Automation
STEP 02

Copy — then tap C again

Press Ctrl+C, then tap C once more within half a second. A floating popup snaps open with your captured text. The second letter is configurable.

CtrlC then C
STEP 03

Pick a preset, replace

Choose Fix Grammar, Make Formal, Lookup — anything. Hit Enter to rewrite, Enter again to replace the text right where it was.

02 — Built for the keyboard

Quiet, fast, and never in your way.

Designed for non-native English speakers who type all day. Mouse-free, account-free, and entirely under your control.

Works everywhere

UI Automation captures text from any app — Outlook, Word, Slack, Notion, browsers, even legacy apps. Clipboard fallback included.

Zero setup

The built-in provider works the moment you install — no account, no API key, no model picking. A 14-day free trial starts automatically.

Streaming rewrites

Watch the improved text arrive word by word. Cancel anytime if the rewrite goes sideways.

Side-by-side diff

See exactly what changed. Deletions struck through, additions highlighted. No mystery rewrites.

Built-in dictionary lookup

Select any word and hit Lookup: definitions, IPA pronunciation, example sentences, a memory aid to make it stick — and a speaker button to hear it aloud.

Learn from your writing

Every rewrite and lookup is saved on your machine — review past results, spot your patterns, and memorize new words. Nothing leaves your PC.

Dark, light, or system

A phosphor-on-black dark theme and a warm paper light theme, across the popup, settings, and learn. Follows Windows by default — just like this page.

Undo replace

Replaced the wrong thing? One click restores the original text in the source app — instantly.

Keyboard-first

Stay on the home row. Tap a number or step through presets with ← →, press ↵ to rewrite or replace, and Esc to dismiss — your hands never leave the keyboard.

Automatic updates

Signed releases update themselves in the background — you always run the latest version without hunting for a new installer.

03 — Presets

Eight presets out of the box. Customize freely.

Seven rewrite presets with carefully tuned prompts — edit them, add your own, or delete what you don't use — plus the built-in Lookup dictionary.
